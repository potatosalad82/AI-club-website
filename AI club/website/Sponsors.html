Launch, July 7, 2018

Welcome to the grand opening of Potato Schools. This is a project from Potato Industries with the goal to teach others coding from scratch to computer learning algorithms. We will release one group of lessons every month or so eventually creating a curiculm that spans multiple diffrent coding languages as well as 3D modelling, photoshop, and more. If you enjoy our lessons please donate so that we can keep everything free. If we do not recive many donations we will try to still keep everything free but maybe release stuff early to those who donate. Anyway we are also going to open hours where you can live chat with a coach for help and maybe even open up in person classes someday.

Please enjoy this website and if you have any suggestions about content or even if you just want to talk visit our contact page and send us an email. Also please check out our other website gameportals.org
